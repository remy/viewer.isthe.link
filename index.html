IMAGE VIEWER

For viewing directories of images, and ideal for casting to a Chromecast

Usage:

  • Drag and drop directories (or click here) to browse images
  • Cursor keys ⇠ and ⇢ to navigate previous and next
  • Click anywhere to goto next
  • Double click for fullscreen or f – esc to return to window
  • :numeric: + enter to jump to image
  • d download the current image
  • s star an image to download a zip of all starred images
  • a to toggle autoplay
